## What is StoragePulse?

StoragePulse is a Windows desktop application designed to give small and medium-sized businesses complete, real-time visibility over their warehouse operations. It replaces messy spreadsheets and outdated stock books with a clean, intuitive interface that keeps everything — products, suppliers, orders, and stock movements — in one place.

The application is built for speed and clarity: you open it, and within seconds you know exactly how many products are running low, how much your total stock is worth, and which purchase orders are still pending.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Live Dashboard
Get an instant snapshot of your warehouse health. The dashboard shows:
- Total number of products and active suppliers
- Overall stock value (in local currency)
- Count of low-stock and out-of-stock products
- Pending purchase orders at a glance
- A live feed of recent stock movements

### 📦 Product Management
Maintain a full product catalogue with codes, categories, units of measure, unit prices, and configurable reorder points. Know when to restock before you run out.

### 🏭 Supplier Management
Keep detailed supplier records including contact names, phone, email, address, and tax numbers — everything you need to place an order without hunting through notebooks.

### 🔄 Stock Movements
Log every stock entry and exit with document numbers, movement dates, quantities, and optional reference notes. Build a complete audit trail of what came in and went out.

### 🛒 Purchase Orders
Create purchase orders through a step-by-step wizard, add line items, set expected delivery dates, and track status from *Draft* through to *Received*. Full order history is always available.

### 📈 Reports
Generate and export inventory reports to Excel — useful for periodic stock takes, financial reviews, or sharing with management.

### ⚙️ Company Settings
Personalize the application with your company name, address, phone, email, and tax number. Settings are reflected across reports and printed documents.

## 🖥️ Who is it for?

StoragePulse is ideal for:
- Small warehouses and storage facilities
- Retail businesses managing physical stock
- Manufacturing operations tracking raw materials
- Any team that needs a simple, offline-first inventory tool without cloud subscriptions or per-seat licensing

| **Dashboard** | Real-time KPIs, low-stock alerts, and recent movements |
| **Products** | Full product catalogue with categories and reorder thresholds |
